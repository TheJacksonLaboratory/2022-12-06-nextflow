Who: The course is aimed at students and other researchers and it is intended for those who want to learn how to develop Nextflow pipelines. Some reasons Nextflow is popular include its provision of good parallelization, scalability, structure/compartmentalization, ability to resume failed jobs, and portability across a range of run-time environments (e.g. localhost, SLURM, GCP, AWS, Kubernetes). You should have basic bash skills to succeed in this course. In this class, we will use the Nextflow programming language to develop a simple bioinformatics pipeline.
